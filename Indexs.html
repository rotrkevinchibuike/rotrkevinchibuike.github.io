ABOUT CHIBUIKE

Chibuike Johnbosco Kevin Orjioke was born into the family of Emmanuel Orjioke in April 10, 1992. He hails from Mmaku in Awgu LGA of Enugu State. He is the first son of the family of four. He is Christian and single. Chibuike Johnbosco Kevin Orjioke attended the following higher institutions Enugu State College of Education Technical, Enugu and National Open University of Nigeria, Enugu Study Center.
